One Piece

8.6

Monkey D. Luffy, a carefree yet powerful teen with a superhuman ability, survives a whirlpool and ends up on a pirate-attacked ship. Inspired by the legendary Pirate King Gol D. Roger, who revealed the location of the One Piece treasure, Luffy sets out to become the next Pirate King. Despite lacking a crew and proper ship at first, his determination and spirit lead him to face countless dangers with a smile, while gathering unique companions for an unforgettable adventure across the seas.
